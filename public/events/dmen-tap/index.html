Secret Furry Gamers Meeting (Board Game Bar)

When: Monday, June 9, 2025, 5:00 PM – 9:00 PM

Location: Secret Entrance (Dmen-tap Google Maps link)

Venue: Dmen Tap

Cost: Free for non-dragons, otherwise 20% hoard-gains tax above each metric tonne of gold.
