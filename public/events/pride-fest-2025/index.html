Furcago Pride Fest Meetup

When: Saturday, June 21, 2025, 5:00 PM – 9:00 PM

Location: Halstead & Addison

Venue: Public Venue - Organized by Northalsted

Cost: Free. Donations will be asked for at the entrance but it is not required

Age Rating: All Ages

Capacity: No Limit

Accessibility: Wheelchair accessible, noise level and crowd will be significant, we will not have a designated place to sit

Details: Join Furcago for a meetup at Chicago Pride Fest! 🏳️‍🌈🐺✨ We’re gathering suiters and furry friends for a short Pride Walk & dance around the festival grounds — bringing joy, queer visibility, and fuzzy hugs to the heart of Boystown. Whether you’re in full suit, partial, or just yourself, you’re welcome. We’ll meet at Halsted & Addison (see map link) at 5pm. It will be busy & there will be other people drinking, please suit with what you feel comfortable with risking a spilled drink on.
